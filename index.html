1. CANCELLATION POLICY

If the client cancels the scheduled moving service less than 5 days before of the agreed date, a $100 cancellation fee will be charged.

The cancellation fee will be automatically charged to the credit card provided by the Client at the time of booking.

2. SHORT-NOTICE RESCHEDULING FEES

If the Client requests to reschedule the move less than 2 days before of the original move day a $50 rescheduling fee will apply.

Rescheduling is subject to availability, and Sven Moving Corp cannot guarantee the requested new date/time.

If rescheduling is not possible and the Client cancels instead, the cancellation fee will still be applied.

The Client may reschedule the move a maximum of two (2) times.

If the Client has already rescheduled once and then decides to cancel, the $100 cancellation fee still apply.
